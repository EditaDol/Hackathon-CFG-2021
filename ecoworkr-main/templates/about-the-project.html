About the project

ecoworkr is our project for the Code First Girls and Beazley 2021 Hackathon “Hack Your Carbon Footprint”, which challenged us to create a product, app, or website that will empower employers, employees, and freelancers to slash their carbon footprint. We created this website off the fact that the way we work has rapidly changed and will only continue to do so. Remote and hybrid working is becoming the norm. Companies are embracing this due to the benefits that workplace flexibility has on team productivity and attracting talent. The world saw a massive decrease in global carbon emissions when lockdowns forced us all to stay home, with no commute to the office and no overseas travelling. Flexible working can help keep these emissions down. However, with tech being an integral part of most of our everyday lives, our attention must now turn to our digital carbon footprint. ecoworkr empowers individuals and companies alike to become more aware of the environmental impact that the online tools we use everyday have on the world we live in. We think this knowledge should be free and accessible to all. So, while we are currently a work in progress, ecoworkr will forever remain an accessible tool for all. If you'd like to reach out for more information, you can reach us on LinkedIn, or use the contact form below.
