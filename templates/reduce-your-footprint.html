For remote workers and freelancers

Turn off your camera unless it adds value to your call

Unsubscribe from unwanted email subscriptions

Lower your monitor brightness

For employers

Choose a video conferencing provider for your company with a commitment to using 100% renewable energy (like Google Meet)

Regularly clean out company cloud storage drives of unused files. As well as reducing carbon emissions from old files, it will lower cloud storage costs, and less clutter = more efficient teams.

Adopt a Lifecycle Management Process in your business and adhere to circular economy principles

Save bigger meetings for days most people are in the office. Avoid scheduling unnecessary meetings for what could be a succint email or message.
